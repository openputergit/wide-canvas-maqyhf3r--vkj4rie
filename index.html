Welcome to CampusXchange

Your one-stop marketplace for campus commerce. Buy, sell, and exchange items and services with your fellow students. Earn cafeteria credits through donations!

About CampusXchange

Our Mission

CampusXchange aims to create a sustainable and collaborative marketplace within the campus community, promoting resource sharing and environmental consciousness.

Key Features

  • Buy and sell used items
  • Offer and find student services
  • Earn cafeteria credits through donations
  • Secure messaging system
  • Admin-verified listings

How It Works

Simply create an account, browse listings, or post your own items and services. Our platform ensures safe transactions and community-focused exchange.
